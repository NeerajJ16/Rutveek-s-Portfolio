01Tutor of
the Month

I am incredibly honored to be selected as the Tutor of the Month at the Academic Advancement Center at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. This recognition means a lot to me, as it reflects the appreciation and positive feedback from the students I have had the privilege to work with. Teaching is more than just a job for me; it's a passion. As an instructor for engineering courses like CATIA and MATLAB, I strive to inspire, support, and guide each student, helping them reach their full potential. Knowing that my efforts have made a positive impact on their academic journey is deeply rewarding. A heartfelt thank you to all the students who took the time to share their feedback and recognize my dedication. Your success is my greatest motivation, and I look forward to continuing this rewarding journey with you all.

02 Leadership
Conference

What does it take to become a leader? Nothing extraordinary, yes, that's right. Every individual is a leader in one way or the other. It's the ability of making decisions considering everyone's perspective is what makes a good leader. Attending the Florida International leadership conference over the span of 3 days included enjoyment, learning, and bonding. From breakout sessions to bonfire sessions, it was an immense experience at the conference. I would like to thank Gabriela Giraldo for all the efforts she put in for us and making sure the experience was the best one. 03CRLA
Certified Tutor

Beginning the semester, in January 2024, I started my tutoring journey at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. With my prior experience as a tutor, my passion for teaching developed. 3D Computer Aided Designs (CAD) being one of my strong pillars, I decided to help and encourage others in the field of CAD modelling. Teaching Engineering graphics have been my favourite part of the semester. I am pleased to announce that I am a CRLA certified tutor with a Level 1 certification. Additionally, I started teaching MATLAB to students. I believe that base concepts should be crystal clear which helps to bring imagination to reality. I would like to thank the Academic Advancement Center at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University for all the glorious things they do for students.

04Mechanical Engineer
Dassault Systems

I received the title of 'Mechanical Designer' by DassaultSystems. I successfully completed both of the certification examinations. In the month of March, I accomplished Part design and Assembly design certifications, by achieving 155/155 and 150/155 respectively. This recognition enhances my passion and skills towards CAD and it also powers my urge to learn and grow more. I aim to be more involved and curious which will help me learn more.
